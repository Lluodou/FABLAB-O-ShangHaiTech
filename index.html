The goal of this assignment is to design and make some parts of splicing toy.

Design

The first step is to design it. First, we design a part that have many slots, which means this part is used to be a connector, connecting other parts together. First, we draw a large circle, then we draw a slot, then we copy it, and rotate it for 45°, then we choose two slots, and rotate it for 90°, and choose four slots and rotate it for 180°. Finally, we have 8 slots.
